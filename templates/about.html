About Me

My name is Luuk De Werd, I'm a photographer.

I am an allround photographer from the city of Tilburg, The Netherlands, and I capture calmness and pureness amidst the entirety. My work can be described best as serene and minimalistic with a lot of color and contrast.

So far I've had the opportunity to exhibit my work on several occasions in Tilburg, with most recently my participation in the Kunstscene hosted by Ateliers Tilburg. Here I exhibited my favorite photos in the office of De Bont Makelaardij.
